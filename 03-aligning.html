Culture clash

If you’re a bioinformatician - you may be wondering why I’m not using the words ‘mapping’ reads here. Well, the geneticists in the crowd have a much older technique called mapping that does something completely different. I have honestly had conversations where it has been insisted that I can’t possibly map read.

If you’re a geneticist - bioinformaticians claim they can do amazing things with reads, mapping them down to the single read level! Of course they usually just mean they’re aligning them.
